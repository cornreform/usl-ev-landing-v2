# Manim Hero Animation Plan

## Concept: "Convergence"
抽象幾何動畫，代表高科技與環保嘅融合。柔和、流暢、舒適。

## Duration
30 seconds loop

## Color Palette
- Background: #0D0D0D
- Primary: #00A8E0 (Electric Blue)
- Secondary: #00D4AA (Teal/Green)
- White highlights: #FFFFFF at low opacity

## Visual Elements

### Layer 1: Flowing Lines
- 3-5 horizontal flowing lines
- Very slow horizontal movement (right to left, then reverse)
- Lines have gradient from blue to teal
- Low opacity (0.3-0.5)
- Creates sense of energy flow

### Layer 2: Geometric Particles
- Small circles (3-8px radius)
- Floating slowly in random patterns
- Color: blue and teal
- Opacity: 0.2-0.4
- Subtle pulsing (scale animation)

### Layer 3: Central Geometric Shape
- Abstract hexagonal or circular form
- Centered, low opacity
- Slow rotation
- Electric glow effect

### Layer 4: Energy Trails
- Thin lines that streak across
- Follow curved paths
- Fade in and out
- Suggest speed without being aggressive

## Animation Timing
- All animations use slow easing (sine curves)
- No sudden movements
- Breathing effect on all elements
- 6-8 second animation cycles

## Technical Notes
- Render at 1920x1080
- 30fps
- Output as MP4 with alpha channel if possible
- Fallback: WebM format for web compatibility

## Mood Reference
Lucid Motors ambient backgrounds - minimalist, meditative, but purposeful.
